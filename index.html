About SauceyPlayz

I am SauceyPlayz, an indie video game developer. I have been developing games for many years now, but I have only released one game with another one that is soon to follow. I am inspired by my favorite video games to create experiences for other people to enjoy.

My top 5 favorite games in order are:

  1. Titanfall 2
  2. Xenoblade Chronicles 2
  3. Pokemon Heartgold
  4. Pokemon Legends Arceus
  5. Persona 3 Reload

While I can't possibly list every single inspiration I have, these ones are some of my favorites. It is my hope that my games are played and enjoyed by lots of people, and maybe I can inspire someone else.
